Panda

Though it belongs to the order Carnivora, the giant panda is a folivore, with bamboo shoots and leaves making up more than 99% of its diet.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda Melanoleuca
  • Origin: South Central China
  • Diet:Bamboo
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years

Pandas are characterised by large, black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body. The giant panda has luxuriant black-and-white fur. Adults measure around 1.2 to 1.9 metres (3 feet 11 inches to 6 feet 3 inches) long, including a tail of about 10–15 cm (4–6 in), and 60 to 90 cm (24 to 35 in) tall at the shoulder.
